About Me

Tuan Nguyen is a doctoral candidate and research assistant in the Department of Leadership, Policy, and Organizations at Vanderbilt University. He taught middle and high school math for seven years in St. Louis, Missouri before resuming his graduate studies at Peabody. He holds a dual-degree in Math and Physics from the University of Oklahoma and a Master in the Arts of Teaching from Washington University in St. Louis.

Research

Investigating the drivers of resilience, vulnerability, and adaptive capacity and their impacts on social-environmental systems using spatiotemporally explicit modeling and behavior driver simulation methodologies within an interdisciplinary framing.

Teaching

Preparing students for roles as future scientists, engineers, and decision-makers through interdisciplinary, problem-based and investigative learning.
